Description

This recipe comes from TERRI-ANN'S KITCHEN she is a youtuber that post different recipes.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Thigh - 450 gms (1 lb)

    • For the Teriyaki Sauce:

  • Light Soy Sauce- 3 tbsp (Preferably Kikkoman)
  • Sugar- 3 tbsp
  • Apple Cider Vinegar- 1 tsp
  • oil- 1 tbsp
  • Ginger grated- 1 tsp
  • Garlic grated- 1 tsp

    • Other Ingredients:

  • Salt for seasoning- 1/4 tsp
  • Pepper powder for seasoning- 1/4 tsp
  • Oil for pan frying the chicken- 1.5 tbsp
  • Sesame seeds- 1 tsp (as garnish)
  • Spring onion greens, chopped- 2 tbsp (as garnish)

Steps

  1. Cut the boneless thighs into bite sized chunks
  2. Grate the ginger, garlic and prepare the teriyaki sauce
  3. Heat oil in a pan and then add the chicken pieces. Season with salt and pepper powder.
  4. Now add the Teriyaki Sauce (prepared earlier), give a mix and cook on medium heat for 3-4mins till the sauce is absorbed and coats the chicken pieces.
  5. Garnish with the sesame seeds and chopped spring onion greens.
